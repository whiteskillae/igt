🇦🇪 UAE NURSING SELECTION GUIDE

Complete A-Z Career Blueprint

Your definitive guide to becoming a Registered Nurse in the United Arab Emirates. From DHA licensing to your first day at the hospital.

Phase 01

Academic & Clinical Eligibility

To practice as a nurse in the UAE, you must meet the stringent requirements set by the Dubai Health Authority (DHA) or Department of Health (DOH) Abu Dhabi.

  • Degree: B.Sc Nursing or GNM (3+ years)
  • License: Active nursing license from home country
  • Experience: 2+ Years Post-Registration Experience
  • English: Professional Proficiency required

Specialized High-Demand Roles

ICU / Critical Care Specialist
Neonatal (NICU) Veteran
Emergency & Trauma (ER)
Operating Theater Specialist
Pediatric Nursing
Phase 02

The DHA Licensing Workflow

01

Credentialing

Submission of academic documents to DataFlow for primary source verification (PSV).

02

Eligibility

Receiving the DHA Eligibility Letter after successful verification of clinical experience.

03

Prometric Exam

Booking and passing the official DHA examination at an authorized testing center.

04

Placement

Activating your license upon receiving a verified offer from a premium UAE hospital.
