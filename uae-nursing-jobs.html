🇦🇪 UAE NURSING SELECTION GUIDE

Complete A-Z Career Blueprint

Your definitive guide to becoming a Registered Nurse in the United Arab Emirates. From DHA licensing to your first day at the hospital.

Phase 01

Clinical Eligibility

To practice in the UAE, you must meet requirements set by the Dubai Health Authority (DHA) or Department of Health (DOH) Abu Dhabi.

  • Degree: B.Sc Nursing or GNM
  • Active Home Country License
  • 2+ Years Experience
  • English Proficiency

High-Demand Roles

ICU / Critical Care
Neonatal (NICU)
Emergency & Trauma
Operating Theater
Phase 02

The Licensing Workflow

01. Credentialing

Document submission to DataFlow for primary source verification (PSV).

02. Eligibility

Receiving the DHA Eligibility Letter after verification of experience.

03. Prometric

Booking and passing the official DHA examination at authorized centers.

04. Placement

Activating your license upon receiving a verified offer from a hospital.

Phase 03

Package & Benefits
