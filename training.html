Training & Support

Skills and preparation are the foundation of a successful international nursing career. We guide candidates through the essential training and licensing preparation for their target destination.

Preparation Pathways

Areas of Guidance & Support

Licensing Exam Preparation

Comprehensive guidance and resources for licensing exams across all five destination countries.

  • DHA, DOH, HAAD, MOH — UAE
  • QCHP — Qatar
  • AHPRA — Australia
  • Approbation — Germany

Dataflow Verification

Support in understanding and completing the Dataflow background screening process required for Gulf placements.

  • Step-by-step guidance
  • Document preparation support
  • Application review

Communication Skills

Practical coaching on professional communication, medical English, and employer presentation skills.

  • Mock interview sessions
  • Medical English practice
  • Presentation coaching

Career Readiness

Career planning sessions to help candidates identify the right path and prepare for the international transition.

  • Career goal setting
  • Country selection guidance
  • Transition preparation

Language Training

Information on German language courses (A2 to B2 level) and English proficiency (IELTS/OET).

  • German A2 to B2 guidance
  • IELTS / OET prep support
  • Resource recommendations

Pre-Departure Support

Briefings and guidance for a successful relocation and first-day readiness at your work destination.

  • Travel & relocation help
  • Destination orientation
  • Professional onset support
