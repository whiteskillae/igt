UAE — United Arab Emirates

Required: DHA | DOH | HAAD | MOH

The UAE is one of the highest-demand markets for international nurses, with roles across government hospitals, private clinics, and specialist homecare facilities.

  • Full Relocation Support
  • Permanent visa sponsorship
  • Shared/Private Accommodation
  • Full Medical Insurance
  • Licensing & Exam Prep
  • Career advancement path
🇶🇦

Qatar

Required: QCHP Licensing

Qatar's healthcare sector continues to expand rapidly. International nurses are in high demand for hospitals, specialty clinics, and ongoing national development projects.

  • Excellent career development
  • State-of-the-art facilities
  • Comprehensive housing allowance
  • Relocation for entire families
🇦🇺

Australia

Required: AHPRA Registration

Australia offers an outstanding healthcare system, high standards of living, and a clear pathway to permanent residency for qualified professional nurses.

  • Professional Career Growth
  • Permanent Residency support
  • Excellent work-life balance
  • Advanced clinical training
🇩🇪

Germany

Required: Approbation License

Germany has a significant and growing shortage of healthcare professionals, making it one of the most accessible and structured international pathways for European-standard careers.

  • Language course sponsorship
  • EU standard job security
  • Full retirement benefits
  • Family reunion support
🇬🇪

Georgia

Required: Ministry of Health

Georgia offers an accessible and beginner-friendly entry point for nurses seeking international experience, with a straightforward licensing process and growing healthcare sector.

  • Beginner-friendly entry path
  • Fast-track licensing procedure
  • Low initial relocation costs
  • gateway to wider EU markets
