Step 01

Professional Eligibility

Dubai's Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) maintains high standards. Before applying, verify you meet these criteria:

  • Driving license (Manual/Auto)
  • Basic English proficiency
  • Age: 21 to 45 Years
  • Medical & Physical fitness
