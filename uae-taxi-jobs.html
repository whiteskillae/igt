🇦🇪 DUBAI RTA TAXI SELECTION GUIDE

Professional Driving Excellence

Join the elite RTA network in the world's most dynamic city. We provide a guaranteed pathway for motivated professionals.

Step 01

Professional Eligibility & Requirements

Dubai's Roads and Transport Authority (RTA) maintains high standards. Before applying, verify you meet these criteria:

  • Home country driving license (Manual/Auto)
  • Basic English speaking & reading
  • Age Profile: 21 to 45 Years
  • Clean background & Medical fitness
Step 02

Earning Structure & Financial Benefits

We believe in rewarding hard work. Your income in Dubai is limited only by your dedication.

Commission Split

Drivers receive a significant percentage of the daily fare revenue. Average monthly earnings range from 3,500 AED to 6,000 AED based on performance and tips.

Training Allowance

During your 3-month RTA license training period, you receive a monthly allowance of 1,500 AED plus company-provided accommodation.

Zero Fleet Costs

The vehicle, uniform, maintenance, insurance, and professional SIM card are all provided at zero cost to you. No hidden deductions.

Step 03

The Deployment Journey

From your first application to hitting the Dubai roads, we guide you through every milestone.

01

Document Screening

Initial check of passport, license, and background suitability.

02

RTA Assessments

English proficiency and basic computer tests at the RTA center.

03

Visa & Schooling

Medical checks, Visa stamping, and 3 months of professional RTA school.

04

On-Road Success

Receiving your modern vehicle and smart meter to begin your shift.
