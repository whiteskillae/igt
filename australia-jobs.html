The 6-Step Recruitment Process

Our transparent process takes you from submission to visa in approx. 3 months.

1

Step 1: Document Submission

  • • Updated CV
  • • Passport Copy
  • • Passport Size Photo
2

Step 2: Assessment (2–5 Days)

Candidate receives detailed company assessment details. Must sign and submit to move to the next stage.

3

Step 3: Offer Contract (7–15 Days)

Candidate receives a 12-page comprehensive company contract. Must be signed and submitted for final processing.

4

Step 4: ITR File Process

After contract signing, the candidate file will be officially processed for ITR (Income Tax Return) registration.

5

Step 5: Medical Process (3–5 Days)

Undergo medical at a Government Approved Hospital and submit reports for visa clearance.

6

Step 6: Work Permit & Visa

Within 5–7 working days after ITR & Medical, your Work Permit & Visa will be issued. Total process: approx. 3 months.
