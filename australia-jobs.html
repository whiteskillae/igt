The Process

6-Step Recruitment Journey

1

Document Submission

Submit your updated CV, Passport Copy, and Passport Size Photograph to begin the evaluation.

2
2-5 Days

Assessment

Receive company assessment details. Candidates must sign and submit the assessment to proceed.

3
7-15 Days

Offer Contract

Receive a comprehensive 12-page company contract. Must be signed and submitted for official processing.

4

ITR File Process

After contract signing, your file will be officially processed for Income Tax Return (ITR) documentation.

5
3-5 Days

Medical Process

Undergo a medical examination at a Government Approved Hospital and submit the final reports.

6
5-7 Days

Work Permit & Visa

Final issuance of your Australia Work Permit and Visa after ITR and Medical clearance.

Total Processing Duration: Approx. 3 Months

Special Note: If the file is rejected for any reason, re-application will be processed free of cost.
